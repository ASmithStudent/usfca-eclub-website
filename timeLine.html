Timeline

Timeline

Students saw a need create the connection between the USF campus and San Francisco entrepreneurs.
August 2015

Entrepreneur's Club Founded

Abey Panigan and Alec Taylor took action and created Entrepreneur's Club to bridge the gap between the University of San Francisco and entrepreneurs in the San Francisco Bay Area.
FALL 2015

First Meeting & 20 Members

Abey and Alec lead the club's first general meeting and welcome the first 20 members.

FALL 2015September 8

Start of Weekly Meetings

In order to connect like-minded entrepreneurs, the Entrepreneur's Club started holding weekly networking meetings.

FALL 2015

Board of Executives Elected

With the growing numbers of individual joining our club, it was seen as necessary to create a board of six members to organize events and fundraising.

FALL 2015

E-club Hits 40 Members & Begins to Tour Startups

With the growing popularity and presence of Entrepreneur's Club on campus, we decide to use our connections to visit Twitter and Spray Networks.

SPRING 2016OCT 15

E-club Hits 65 Members

E-club goes to South by Southwest for the first time, as well as organizing company visits to Google and Calhacks.

FALL 2017

Newsletter and Innovation Center Created

To make sure E-club keeps members involved and in the know about events, E-club started a newsletter. In addition to the newsletter, we created an innovation center to help foster ideas within the club.

FALL 2017

Newsletter and Innovation Center Created

To make sure E-club keeps members involved and in the know about events, E-club started a newsletter. In addition to the newsletter, we created an innovation center to help foster ideas within the club.

SPRING 2018

E-club Intern Program Starts

To ensure longevity, E-club takes in its first 10 interns. After one semester, the interns become part of the board.

FALL 2018

E-club Hits 100 Members and Hosts Fall Events

With the new interns on board, productivity has increased and E-club was able to host a Summer Internship Panel, Fireside Chats with Andrea Barrica and Bevis Cheng.

FALL 2018

Podcast and Instagram Created

With the new interns on board, productivity has increased and E-club was able to host a Summer Internship Panel, Fireside Chats with Andrea Barrica and Bevis Cheng.

FALL 2018

Previous Interns Promoted, New Interns Onboarded

With the great success of the first intern cohort, E-club brings on some new interns. The first cohort of interns are promoted to executive members and choose their own project to lead.

SPRING 2019

Nikhil Vimal Becomes President of E-club

After demonstrating such great value and commitment to the betterment of USF and E-club, Vice President Nikhil Vimal is promoted to the position of president of E-club.

SPRING 2019

E-Club Visits Autodesk and Stanford School of Design

As part of E-Club's continous effort to learn more about entrepreneurship and innovation, executive members organize trips to Stanford School of Design and Autodesk.
